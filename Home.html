El Nido, Palawan, Philippines

El Nido, Palawan, Philippines

I went on vacation to the Philippines last Christmas.

After my last final, we flew the next day to celebrate my first Christmas home in the 10 years I moved to LA. We visited a lot of different places but the islands of Palawan specifically El Nido has been my new favorite since.

The Palawan islands is located in the Visayas region of the Philippines. It has over 45 islands total and a popular tourist destination of the Philippines. It's popular for it's white sandy beaches, amazing nature scenes, and the well known crystal clear blue water with an abundant oceanlife.
