El Nido is Inexpensive

If you're a smart traveler, you can make it inexpensive. Timing it properly that when you visit isn't during peak season, you can get amazing deals on airfare, hotels, and activities.

Hospitable Residents

Majority of the residents are really friendly. Though some may take advantage of foreigners, you just have to be smart about the local prices so they never hike it up for you.

No Hospitals

There are only a few places where you can purchase medicines and small clinics around the island. It is advised to purchase medicine beforehand.

Bring Extra Cash

It is important to bring cash or at least have a card to use that doesn't charge travel fee in order to use it on the island since there is no ATM there. There is one at the airport if you so happen to fly through Puerto Princesa.

Busy Town

Since it's such a popular tourist destination, the island of El Nido is almost busy year round.

Island Hopping is the BEST

Island hopping is one of the best activities to do on the island that will make you fall in love with El Nido. It is also one of the most well known activities to do while visiting.

Foreign Tourists

Surprisngly enough, there is a lot of foreign tourists. While I was there, i met a lot of people from Europe, Asia, US, and more!

Power interruption is normal

Because it is still counted as a province, there are times where power may fluctuate and this is normal.

One of the BEST Islands in the World

Among the beaches of the world, El Nido can be counted as one of the best of them all!
