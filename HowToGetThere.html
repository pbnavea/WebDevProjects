How To Get There

Ways to get there

Direct flight

There are multiple ways to get to El Nido and the fastest yet most expensive way is going straight from Manile to El Nido. according to a travel website called El Nido Paradise mentioned that an airline company called Air Swift have multiple flights to El Nido but it ranges from 7,000-9,000 pesos(approx. $140-$180) per person depending on the season and if they have any special offers.

Plane and Road

Another common way, and one of the cheapest way, is a flight to their local airport in the city of Puerto Princessa. We took this route for our trip since it was cheaper even though it was the one of the longer route. It was an additional 6 hours on the road but we did stop over a lot of popular spots like Baker's Hill where they have amazing baked goods and a lot of the small shops have an overloooking view of the ocean.

By Sea

Another way is getting there by sea. According to El Nido Paradise you are able to buy a ticket for Manila to Coron and then purchase a second ticket from Coron to Palawan so it can take up to 24 hours by sea to get to El Nido. This would be the longest option by far and quite expensive as well.
