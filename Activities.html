Activities

There are multiple activites that you can do while you visit El Nido. There isIsland Hopping, Kayaking,Hiking, Snorkling, and more!

Island hopping

One of the most common activities to do at Palawan is island hopping. You typically hop on boats like the ones I pictured myself above and they can take you to various islands from 5-7 islands, some can be customized, you can either book an entire boat to yourself and your group or be with outher tourists.

Kayaking

Another exciting activity is Kayaking. They typically have the kayaks on this floating store which is basically a boat and they rent it to you. They have designated areas where you can kayak to preserve the ocean environment.

In the picture above, I teamed up with my godbrother as we kayaked to the big and small lagoons. We regret not taking pictures because the views were breathtaking. We were even told that a small area in the small lagoon, there lived baby sharks which was pretty insane knowing how close we were.

Hiking

I personally don't do it often here in LA but hiking there was magnificent. I personally got the opportunity to see such an amazing view such as the image I took of above. Me and my godfamily decided to do a morning hike to get us to wake up a little before another day of adventure.

Snorkling

This HAS to be my favorite activity in the entire trip: snorkling!! At first it was scary because I'm in the middle of the open ocean but once I dove into the water, I was just amazed at what I saw. I've never been so close to a school of fishes, coral reefs that are vibrant in color, and just being at the center of it all was unbelievable! I looked around it was just a whole other world because the sea seemed endless from that one point I was floating from.
